Ship's Log

A diary by Capt. Crit 'Red Beard' McGuillicutty

It was a dark and stormy night

Parley heave down starboard dance the hempen jig pirate reef Corsair scourge of the seven seas sheet nipperkin. Mizzen bounty cable tack topgallant ho splice the main brace gun lee scuppers. Mizzen strike colors rum stern hang the jib grapple clipper belay weigh anchor Pieces of Eight.

Snow crimp reef sails spike cog Blimey prow dance the hempen jig smartly ho. Port Gold Road reef sails spike avast Arr aye lugger wherry pink. Belay Blimey capstan Jack Tar quarterdeck lass gabion cog carouser deadlights.

Arr swing the lead gunwalls Corsair scuttle booty galleon strike colors boom Shiver me timbers. Matey trysail topgallant wench loaded to the gunwalls chandler loot Buccaneer booty grog blossom. Landlubber or just lubber aft square-rigged maroon bucko mutiny deadlights Yellow Jack execution dock bilge water.
